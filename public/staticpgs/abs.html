Abs Exercises

- - -

1. Barbell Floor Wiper

Directions:
1. Lie with your back flat on the floor and your arms extended holding the barbell above your chest.
2. Keeping your arms fixed straight raise your legs up to the L-shaped position.
3. Lower your leg down to each side then back up without touching the floor.

2. Medicine Ball Slam

Directions:
1. Standing up with your knees slightly bent lift the medicine ball directly over your head with your arms extended.
2. Rise up on the balls of your feet and use your core muscles to throw the ball to the ground as you bend forwards at the waist.
3. Catch the ball and repeat. The motion will not only train your abs but will also give you powerful shoulders.

- - -

3. Side Jackknife

Directions:
1. Lay on your side with your left arm extended out on the floor and you right arm bent to your head with your elbow bent out.
2. Make sure your right leg is on top of your left.
3. Bring your right elbow to your left leg as you raise your body up, contracting your obliques and slowly lower down before swapping sides after reps

-

4. Dragon Flag

Directions:
1. Lie back on the bench with your hands holding the bench behind your head.
2. Bring your knees up to your chest and kick out to the ceiling, raising your body with just your shoulders on the bench.
3. Keep your body straight and as you slowly move down. You'll not only feel the burn in your abs but in your lower back too.

5. Cable Woodchopper

Directions:
1. Set the cable machine to the highest position with and stand side on to the weights with your back to the machine.
2. Grabbing the handle with both hands take a step away from the tower and put your feet shoulder width apart.
3. Extend your arms fully and pull the handle down and across your body whilst rotating your torso.
4. Bend your knees and pivot your back foot and slowly return to the start position, swapping sides after each set.

6. Cocoon

Directions:
1. Lay flat on your back with your arms extended behind your head and your feet slightly off the ground.
2. Pull your knees towards your chest, lift your backside off the floor and lift your arms over your head as you perform a crunch and repeat.

7. Sandbag Sit-Up

Directions:
1. Lie with your back on the ground and you knees bent upwards.
2. Hold a sandbag up above you with both extended arms and crunch forwards as you tense your core so your body performs a V shape with your thighs.
3. Carefully lower down and repeat.

8. Hanging Leg Raise

Directions:
1. Grab a pull-up bar making a V shape with your arms and lower yourself into a dead hang.
2. Put your feet together and raise your legs until they are perpendicular to your torso whilst keeping them straight.
3. Slowly lower back to the starting position.

9. Superman With A Twist

Directions:
1. Lie on your stomach and place your hands on your head.
2. Raise your torso and twist your chest from one side to the other.
3. This move will not only target your abs but can also help combat that dreaded back pain.

10. Barbell Roll Outs

Directions:
1. Load a barbell with 5kg plates and grab the bar with an overhand, shoulder-width grip.
2. Position your shoulders directly over the barbell and slowly roll the bar forwards.
3. Pause, then reverse the move.
4. Roll out to a distance that's challenging, but doesn't force your hips to sag.
